Guide

Introduction

Vis Vires, which in latin stands for "force, power, strength, might and influence", in a webbrowser based strategy game based on medieval times. You build your towns, expand, manage your people and economics and engage various interractions with other players, such as diplomacy and amrket deals, or battle each other.

On the left side of the website, there is your game anvigation menu, from which you can operate. Each of the links is explained in detail below.

Map

Here you can view where your kingdom stretches and also where kingdoms of other players are located. You can also see armies marching through the lands and send your troops on campaign missions, where you can get into battle and take over a town or a specific territory. It's also your tactical guidence assistent.

Economy

Your kingdom has a growing number of people. To manage them efficiently, you are provided with economy link tab. Yout can allocate and reallocate workers among different resource sectors(see !!!), providing how much workers should be gathering a specific resource.

Building

The nature of kingdom is to expand, and what is better way to do it then building new structures? In the building tab you can chose from variety of buildings, which give additional boost to you kingdom and some even have additional features; some of them are needed in order to build other structures or to be able to research something (see research!!!) or to recrute army of some sort. Once built, they can be upgraded, which provides more housing as more of such buildings are built among your empire and thus more workers can for example be occupied.

Technology

When years go by, new inventions come by, which can make life of your citizen easier and thus hapier and more eager to work or your battles more successful. Same as building, they can be researched even further once they are researched once and they are also sometimes necessary for other technologies, buildings and armies to be able to build/research/recrute(see building""").

Army

In order to protect your kingdom and to conquer new lands, you need an army. Here you can choose from variety of armies, each with specific cons and pros. I order to recrute a specific army, you need to build a specific building and/or do a specific research in order to improve their attributes. Once you ahve the army, you can command it with your own will.

Market

Sometimes your kingdom is in shortage of one good and maybe in prosperity of another. In such cases market is the right place to deal with the problem. Here you can exchange goods with other fellow players, with whom you are not in bad relationship, such as war. The exchange rates and details are a part of aggrement of the traders who engage the trade itself.

Diplomacy

There is also a place for political aggrements, that is, diplomacy. Here you can make any sort of written contracts, for example you can sign a truss or declare a war state with one or several kingdoms, or make any kind of pakt.
